Early Teenage - Yaha Se Time Badalne Laga, Or Aaj Bhi Iska Asar Hai.

Class 9 to Class 11 = Is Point Se Meri Priorities Change Hona Start Hui, Mera Ladkiyon Ki Traf Attraction Bdhna Start Hua, Thode Bure Dost Bhi Banna Start Hue (Jo Nashe Se Addicted The Like - Cigarette, Drinking)

Starting Me Maza Aane Laga, Ladkiyon Se Baat Karke, Mujhe Lagne Laga Tha Mai Ek Hero Type Hu Or Mai Jis Ladki Ko Propose Karunga Vo Meri Girlfriend Ban Jayegi. ( Toh Maze Maze Me Maine 2 Ladkiyo Pe Try Kiya Lekin Unhone Bahut Buri Trah Ke Se Reject Kardiya), Chalo Koi Nahi, Ek ladki Or Thi Jo Kafi Acche Se Baat Karti Thi Mujhse, Mujhe Pakka Yakeen Tha Ki Vo Haa Kardegi, Lekin Usne Bhi Mana Kar diya.

Jo Nashe Wala Dost Tha, Usse Baat Or Badne Lagi Thi, Us Time Tak (Vo Padhne Me Weak Tha)

Ab Dhere Dhere, Uski Sangati Ka Asar Mujhpe Hua, Lekin Maine Kabhi Bhi Nashe Ko Hath Nahi Lagaya Uski Trah Like (Cigarette, Or Drinking) Balki Maine Isse Bhi Bade Nashe Ko Touch Kar diya Galti Se Or Uska Naam Tha (PORN)

Is Point Se Mera OCD (Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder) Bhi Heavily Start Ho Chuka Tha, Khair Ye Tha Toh Pehle Se Bhi Tha Lekin Effect Neglible Tha, But Ab Har Time Useless Thoughts Aane Start Ho Gaye The.

Late Teenage - Ye Peak Point Hai Jaha Se Sari Problems Start Hui.

Class 12 to Second Year = Mere Gharwale Bahut Jyada Bharosa Karte The Mujhpe Isliye Unhone Mujhe Aakash Coaching Me Admission Karwaya, Or Vo Jo Mera Nashe Wala Dost Thana, Vo Bhi Usme Hi Tha ( Ab Hum Waha Only Bakchodi Karte, Or night Me Heavy Porn Consume Karta, Ye Cheez Maine Us Time Pe Kari Jaha Mere Parents Ko Mujhpe Bahut jyada Bharosa Tha.)

Mai Dekh Raha Tha Ki Mere Gharwale Bahut Mehnat Kar Rahe Hai, Toh Mujhe Kuch Support Karna Chahiye, Toh Mere Bank Account Me Jitne Bhi Paise The Maine Sab Jaldi Grow Karne Ke Chakkar Me Lose Kar Diya ( Alag Alag Tariko Se)

Khair Jaise Taise, College Mila ( Bahut Umeed thi ki College Me Mehnat Karke Gharwalo Ko Thoda Support Karo Lekin Abhi Tak Mera Ek Bhi Addiction Kam Nahi Hua.)

Aaj Bhi Vo Nashe Wala Dost Sath Hai, Porn Just Likhte Likhte Dekha Hai, Skills Kuch Bhi Nahi Seekhi, Abhi Tak Placement Ki Tayyari Zero Hai ( Obsessive-Compulsive-Disorder ) Aaj Bhi Apne Sath Hai.

Aisi Khayi Me Gir Chuke Hu, Jaha Andhkar Ke Alawa Or Kuch Nahi Dikhta, Mere Sabhi Dost Jo Mujhse Bahut Peeche Hue Karte The, Aaj Bahut Aage Hai ( Mai Apne Na Mann Ko Theek Kar Paya, Or Na Addictions Se Jeet Paya.)
